## World Hangover Day

World Hangover Day is celebrated on the only appropriate date, i.e. after the New Year's Eve party. On January 1, we may need some advice on how to get through this unpleasant situation.

[Learn more](https://www.kalbi.pl/swiatowy-dzien-kaca)

## World Day of Peace

The holiday was established on the initiative of the French philosopher and traveler Raoul Follereau by Pope Paul VI in 1967. On this occasion, each year the Pope issues a message on peace to all people of good will.

[Learn more](https://www.kalbi.pl/swiatowy-dzien-pokoju)

## Public Domain Day

This holiday is intended to draw attention to the role of the public domain in building culture and access to information. This holiday is closely related to the concept of copyright and it is no coincidence that it is celebrated on January 1.

[Learn more](https://www.kalbi.pl/dzien-domeny-publicznej)
