## World Introvert Day

For many people, a relaxing evening in an armchair with a book and a cup of delicious, hot tea is a nice break from their busy lifestyle. However, there are people who spend their time this way almost every day and feel great about it! Introverts, because we are talking about them, celebrate their day on January 2. It's worth making them happy on this occasion and giving them a book or a large mug of tea. However, the best gift will be to understand their needs and respect their unique disposition.

## Puff Day

Let those of us who don't like desserts cast the first stone! Chocolates, cakes and candies are delicious sweets that everyone enjoys. One of the sweet delicacies even had its own day. It's about Puff Day, which we celebrate on January 2. The creamy, melt-in-your-mouth French cookie has crowds of fans all over the world. At the beginning of the year, you can eat cream puffs with impunity on their holiday, and the calories will still be burned while implementing your New Year's resolutions!
